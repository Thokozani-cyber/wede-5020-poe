Our Story

Founded in 2015, Paws & Claws Animal Rescue started as a small foster network and has grown into a community‑driven rescue saving over 500 animals each year. We are a no‑kill shelter focused on rehabilitation and adoption.

Mission Statement

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned and abused animals while promoting responsible pet ownership through education and community outreach.

Vision Statement

A world where every companion animal has a safe, loving, and permanent home.

Our Team

Led by founder Sarah Johnson and supported by 20+ dedicated volunteers, we work tirelessly to give animals a second chance.
